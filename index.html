HOW SHOULD I STORE MY TREATS? +
To maintain freshness and potency, keep cookies and brownies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days, or refrigerate for longer shelf life. Ice creams and popsicles must be kept frozen until you're ready to consume them.
I'M A FIRST TIMER, WHAT DO YOU RECOMMEND? +
Welcome to the club! We highly recommend starting slow. Have half a cookie or a small scoop of ice cream. Wait at least 60-90 minutes before considering having more, as our treats can take some time to take full effect. Consume with caution!
